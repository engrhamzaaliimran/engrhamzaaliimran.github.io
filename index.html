Engr. Hamza Ali Imran

Hamza Ali Imran received a BS degree in electrical engineering from the National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (NUCES-FAST), Islamabad, Pakistan, in 2018, and an MS degree in computer science from the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (SEECS) at the National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Islamabad, in 2022.

He served at Emumba Pvt. Ltd., Islamabad, on different teams from December 2018 until March 2024. He also worked as a research assistant at the Embedded Systems and Pervasive Computing (EPIC) Laboratory of NUCES-FAST from 2017 to 2018, where he focused on high-performance computing. He is a Udemy instructor with over 5000 students worldwide.
